About The Island

The Site of Cockatoo island first began as a convict settlment from 1839 to 1869, And a repairing facility from the years 1857 and 1991. Between the years of 1871 and 1911 the island served as an educational institute for boys and girls. In 1913 the island became the official dockyard for the Australian Navy.
